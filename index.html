What is the best day to go out?

And where to go?

These are questions you dont have to answer anymore, because from now on an neuronal network will decide for you.
At least as you like the same restaurans as I do :).

Below you will find a table with a few of my favorite restaurans in Berlin and the perfect day to go there. Once this page is loaded the neuronal network is trained to learn from that data which restaurant is the best to go to on which day of the week or the other way around. The training stats are shown below as soon as you hit the "Go Out"-Button. An error rate close to 0 means that the learning process was successful. The Iterations say how many times the set of data has passed the neurons. Once you decide to go out, the trained neuronal network is started to find the most suitable answer to your question.
